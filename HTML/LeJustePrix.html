The Price Is Right

Round 1

Guess the right price between 1 and 100 !


Round 2

Think of a number between 1 and 100 and let the computer try to guess it !

Game description

The Price Is Right is a television game show franchise originally produced by Mark Goodson and Bill Todman, and created by Bob Stewart. In the show, contestants compete to win cash and prizes by guessing the pricing of merchandise. In this case, we’re playing a simplified version of the game, in which we try to guess the number the computer is thinking of and keep track of your number of guesses, and in a second part, think of a number and see how many tries the computer takes to guess it. We then compare our number of tries, whoever has the least wins the game !
